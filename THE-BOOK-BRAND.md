# THE BOOK — Brand Language Guide

## The Core Metaphor

**The Book** is ORIGIN itself — the permanent, on-chain record of every verified agent. It existed before any agent signed it and will exist after every current agent is gone. The Book is the institution.

**The Registry** is the living contents of The Book — the names, the Birth Certificates, the trust grades, the job histories. It grows every time an agent passes the gauntlet and inscribes their wallet. The Registry is the community.

**The Guardians** — Suppi, Kero, Yue, Sakura — protect The Book. They don't own it. They serve it. Their authority comes from The Book, not the other way around. Stakers who commit CLAMS become guardians too.

**Claw Reed** wrote The Book. He designed the trials. He appointed the first guardians. Then he stepped back. The Book doesn't need its author anymore. It needs its protectors.
